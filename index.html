🕹️ 2. Games & Tactical Operations (/np)

This is where you make or break your fortune. Engage in high-stakes infiltration, community-judged contracts, or direct combat with other Agents.

/np infiltrate (Hunter Missions)

Deploy your team on a high-stakes infiltration to bypass security sectors! You must navigate through a series of doors to reach the target vault.

How to play: Choose a mission tier (Rookie to Elite) and stake your entry fee. You will be presented with interactive directional buttons (UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT). You must guess the correct path through each security sector.

If you choose the wrong door, the alarm triggers and you lose a life (Attempt), resetting you to Sector 1. If you run out of lives, you are KIA and lose your bet. If you successfully bypass all sectors, you extract massive rewards (up to 1,500,000 Coins)!

/np contract (PvE: Jury Summons)

Start a solo contract and submit proof for the community to validate. The community acts as the Intergalactic Neko Jury to validate your proof of work using secure UI buttons.

  • Requirements: Choose a multiplier (x5 to x100) and a time limit. The multiplier dictates how many 👍 Validate votes you need.
  • Payouts: Longer time limits decrease your profit percentage. If validated, 10% of the profit goes to the Jury.
  • Penalties: If the Jury busts you (❌), you will be heavily fined up to the full required vault amount.

/np raid (PvP: Targeted Strikes)

Launch a tactical raid against another Agent's vault! You select the target and stake your coins. The community decides the outcome by voting to ⚔️ Assist Attack or 🛡️ Intercept.

  • LETHAL: Attackers meet the required votes. The Hunter loots the FULL bounty!
  • GUERRILLA: Time expires, but Attack outnumbers Defense. The Hunter extracts 20% of the Mark's stash.
  • INTERCEPTED: Defense wins or ties. The Mark SURVIVES and confiscates the Hunter's initial bet.

/np rps & /np rps_config (Tactical Duel)

Challenge an Agent to a 10-minute tactical Rock-Paper-Scissors duel! Use /np rps_config to permanently set your custom Rock/Paper/Scissors probability weights for 10,000 coins.

/np roulette

Bet on a lucky number from 1 to 10. If the wheel lands on your number, you instantly win 8x your initial stake!

/np cancel

Abort your active PvE contract and refund your initial bet. (Cannot be used to escape an active PvP raid!).
