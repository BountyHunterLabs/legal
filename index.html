🏹 Bounty Hunter Bot: Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last Edited: February 16, 2026

🛡️ 1. Minimal Data Collection (Zero Personal Data)

We strictly collect ONLY your numeric Discord User ID and Server ID to manage your virtual balance, stats, and active bounties. We DO NOT collect, store, track, or process your username, avatar, email, or any personal information.

👁️‍🗨️ 2. Why We Collect It & The "Blind Leaderboard" Privacy Feature

This basic numeric data is required to track your progress, wins/losses, and rank on the global leaderboard. Because we strictly refuse to save your username, players from other servers will only see your identity as "@Unknown User" when using the /bh global command. Your real identity is 100% protected and naturally hidden by Discord, being visible only to users who already share a server with you.

🤝 3. Third Parties & Security

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties. All data stays securely within our private database and is used exclusively for the bot's internal economy mechanics.

🗑️ 4. Data Deletion & Control

Because we don't store personal data, your presence in our database is virtually anonymous outside of your shared servers. However, if you want your numeric data completely wiped, please contact the developer via email or use the /bh cancel command to clear any active tasks.

📜 Terms of Service (Rules of the Hunt)

By using Bounty Hunter, you agree to play fair. Exploiting bugs, using macro scripts/auto-clickers, or manipulating the voting system with fake accounts is strictly prohibited. We reserve the right to reset balances or blacklist users who attempt to break the economy.
