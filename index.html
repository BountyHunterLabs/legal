Contract Mechanics & Voting Requirements

To initiate a contract, you must stake a minimum of 500 credits. You choose a multiplier ranging from x5 to x100. The chosen multiplier directly dictates the number of community reactions required.

  • The Formula: Required Votes = Multiplier + 1.
  • Note: The bot's automated reaction and the contract initiator's reaction do not count toward the total. Therefore, an x5 multiplier requires exactly 6 valid community votes to pass.

Time Limits vs. Payout Scaling

When creating a contract, you must select a time limit. Longer durations make it easier to gather the required votes, but they heavily penalize your final payout percentage. If your contract is validated, 10% of the profit is distributed equally among the jurors who voted.

  • x10 Multiplier: Requires 10 votes within 30 minutes.
  • x25 Multiplier: Requires 25 votes within 1 hour.
  • x50 Multiplier: Requires 50 votes within 3 hours.
  • x100 Multiplier: Requires 100 votes within 6 hours.

Rules of Engagement (Payout Logic)

In every Neko Heist, 10% of the stakes are split equally among all hired guns (voters). The outcome of the heist is decided as follows:

  • LETHAL (Hit required ⚔️): The Hunter successfully gathers the required attack votes. The Hunter loots the FULL bounty!
  • GUERRILLA (Time out with ⚔️ majority): Time expires before the required votes are hit, but Attack votes outnumber Defense votes. The Hunter extracts 20% of the Mark's ENTIRE stash.
  • INTERCEPTED (Tie or hit required 🛡️): Defense votes meet the requirement, or the vote ends in a tie. The Mark SURVIVES and strictly CONFISCATES the Hunter's initial bet!

🎁 4. Syndicate Utility Commands (/ns)

/ns giveaway

Host a community giveaway funded by the Syndicate's taxation system.

  • Amount: Minimum 500 credits.
  • Duration: Must be entered in strict seconds. (e.g., 60 = 1 min, 300 = 5 mins, 600 = 10 mins, 1800 = 30 mins, 3600 = 1 hour, 86400 = 24 hours). Tip: Use an online minutes-to-seconds converter for custom times.
  • Reactions: Minimum number of participants required to trigger the payout (min 1).
  • Message: Custom text describing what you are giving away.
Taxation Mechanic: To fund the giveaway, random server members will be incrementally taxed between 2% and 10% of their balance until the giveaway amount is reached. To prevent abuse, giveaways shorter than 5 minutes have a strict cap of 2,500 coins.

/ns stats & /ns invite

  • /ns stats: Displays the total number of members across all servers and the total active server count hosting the bot.
  • /ns invite: Generates the bot invite link. If you invite the bot to a server where you have "Manage Server" permissions, follow up with /ns claim to receive a 100,000 coin bounty.
    Condition: The server must have at least 5 real human members. If the bot is ever kicked from that server, the 100,000 coins will be automatically subtracted from your balance.

/ns advertise

Purchase a dedicated advertising slot on the main Neko Syndicate support server. By spending your credits, your advertisement will be broadcasted to an exclusive ad channel for 6 hours. During this window, all other channel inputs are locked, ensuring maximum visibility for your ad.

Rule: We accept Discord server ads and general promotions. However, promoting illegal content, scams, or phishing links will result in a permanent, irrevocable ban from the Syndicate economy.
